# Cost Optimisation

## SKU Choice

- General Purpose, serverless vCore for personal subscription and lower environments.
- Clear upgrade path to provisioned or Business Critical tiers if IOPS/latency require it.

## Right-Sizing

- Use utilisation metrics and performance baselines to tune vCore counts.
- Consider scale-up for month-end reporting and scale-down afterwards.

## Governance

- Tagging and budgets at resource-group level.
- Alerting on spend anomalies where appropriate.
